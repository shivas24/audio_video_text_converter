 Section 0 of esophs fables, a new revised version by esoph. This sleeper box recording is in the public domain. Preface, the following are some of esophs best-loved fables. The Goose with the Golden Eggs. A certain man had the good fortune to possess a Goose that laid him a Golden Egg every day, but dissatisfied with so slow and encommed, and thinking to seize the whole treasure at once, he killed the Goose and cutting her open, found her just what any other Goose would be. Much once more, and loses all. The town mouse and the country mouse. A country mouse invited a town mouse and intimate friend to pay him a visit and portake of his country fair. As they were on the bare-plowed lands, eating their wheat stock.
